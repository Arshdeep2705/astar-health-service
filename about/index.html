About Astar

Genuine care, delivered by people who truly listen

Astar Health Service is a registered NDIS provider based in Maddingley, Victoria. We exist to help people with disability live the life they choose — with dignity, independence and the right support around them.

Our story

Local roots, a bigger purpose

Astar Health Service was founded on a simple belief: that quality disability support should feel personal, respectful and built entirely around the individual — never one-size-fits-all.

From our home base near Bacchus Marsh, our team supports participants across 17 Victorian suburbs, from metropolitan Melbourne through to Geelong and Ballarat. As a registered NDIS provider, we're held to the National Disability Insurance Scheme's quality and safeguarding standards — and we take them seriously in everything we do.

Every support plan we deliver starts with one question: what matters most to you?

Our mission

To deliver flexible, person-centred NDIS supports that empower Victorians with disability to build independence, pursue their goals and participate fully in their community.

Our vision

An inclusive Victoria where every person — regardless of ability — has genuine choice, control and the support they need to live a full and meaningful life.
