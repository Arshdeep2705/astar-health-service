Our services

A full range of NDIS supports, all in one place

As your needs grow and change, the same trusted Astar team is right beside you. We're registered to deliver these seven NDIS support categories across Victoria.

Daily personal care Independent living Community participation Group activities Life skills Life-stage transitions Travel & transport
Assistance with daily personal activities

Daily Personal Activities

Respectful, hands-on support with the personal routines of everyday life — delivered the way you prefer, in the place you call home.

  • Personal hygiene, showering, grooming and dressing
  • Mobility, transfers and personal safety
  • Medication prompting and wellbeing routines
  • Meal preparation and everyday household tasks

Assistance with daily tasks in a shared living arrangement

Supported Independent Living

Whether you live in a shared home or on your own, we provide the daily assistance that helps you live as independently and safely as possible.

  • Day-to-day help around the home and routines
  • Building skills to manage your household
  • Overnight and 24/7 support options where funded
  • Support that respects shared-home harmony and privacy

Increased social & community participation

Community Participation

Stay connected and do more of what you love. We support you to get out, build friendships and take part in your community with confidence.

  • Social outings, hobbies, sport and recreation
  • Attending appointments, events and local programs
  • Volunteering, classes and pursuing personal interests
  • One-to-one or small-group community access

Group & centre-based activities

Group & Centre Activities

Engaging group programs that build skills, friendships and a real sense of belonging — in a welcoming, supportive environment.

  • Structured, interest-based group activities
  • Skill-building, creative and recreational programs
  • Opportunities to make friends and grow confidence
  • Inclusive activities tailored to all abilities

Development of daily living & life skills

Life Skills Development

Practical, patient coaching that builds the everyday skills and confidence to do more for yourself — at your own pace.

  • Cooking, cleaning and managing a household
  • Budgeting, shopping and money skills
  • Travel training and using public transport
  • Communication, social and decision-making skills

Assistance with life-stage transitions

Life-Stage Transitions

Big life changes are easier with the right support. We help you plan and navigate key transitions so you can move forward with confidence.

  • Moving from school to further education or work
  • Transitioning to more independent living
  • Connecting with services, supports and your community
  • Planning and coordination around major life changes

Assistance with travel & transport

Travel & Transport

Getting there should never be a barrier. We provide safe, reliable transport and travel support so you can keep your commitments and stay active.

  • Transport to appointments, work and study
  • Getting to social, community and program activities
  • Support to build independent travel confidence
  • Friendly, reliable workers who arrive on time
